What is Neuro'Educ ?

Neuro'Educ is an educational initiative focused on increasing awareness and understanding of neurodiversity, particularly autism. Our mission is to provide accessible, evidence-based resources that empower individuals, families, and educators.

About Autism

Autism spectrum disorders (ASD) are a diverse group of conditions. According to the World Health Organization (WHO), they are characterized by varying levels of difficulty with social interaction and communication, as well as atypical patterns of behavior and activity.

Individuals with ASD may experience challenges transitioning between activities, heightened focus on details, and unique responses to sensory input. Understanding and support are essential for creating inclusive environments.

At Neuro'Educ, we strive to break down misconceptions and promote strategies that celebrate neurodiverse perspectives in everyday life and education.
