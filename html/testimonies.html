Living with Autism
“Autism is not a disability, it's a different ability.” — Stuart Duncan

Living with autism is a deeply individual experience, filled with both challenges and unique strengths. Many autistic people have intense focus, honesty, creativity, and a refreshing perspective on the world.

Some may face difficulties with social interaction, communication, or sensory processing. But with early support and inclusion, individuals with autism can thrive in school, at work, and in social settings.

Discover more about autism and support options at NHS Autism Guide or CDC Autism Center.
